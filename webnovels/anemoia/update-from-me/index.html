Update From the Author

Dear readers,

It’s rayes here! First of all, I want to thank all of you, my readers, both of this particular webnovel, and the blog in general. Thank you to all who have sent me feedback and fanmail. And those who pointed out some mistakes in my writing. It truly means a lot to me and I can’t thank you all enough.

Let’s get straight to the point.

As of right now, I am too busy to update this novel according to the original schedule (2 times a week). This was already quite an ambitious goal to begin with, and I intended it to be a test of my time management skills.

I overestimated myself. Don’t get me wrong, I love to write and I am really sad to put this novel on hiatus when I just started it not too long ago. It’s just that there’s too much going on for me right now, managing all my school homework and assignments, uni applications, and my personal life as well.

I hope you guys understand. Please forgive me for being reckless with my scheduling. I will start updating this novel again when I have the time to do so.

Thank you again. I’m truly sorry to those who were eagerly waiting for chapters to come out. I will try and make up for it by putting my all into this novel when I start writing again.

From,
